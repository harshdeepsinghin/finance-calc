About MoneyInFuture

Fast, accurate, and privacy-first financial calculators for Indian retail investors.

What is MoneyInFuture?

MoneyInFuture is a free, open-access collection of personal finance calculators designed specifically for Indian investors. We cover SIP, SWP, EMI, CAGR, XIRR, FIRE, retirement planning, and more — all running entirely inside your browser.

Our Privacy Commitment

Every calculation happens locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your financial data. There are no analytics trackers, cookies, or backend servers involved in running these calculators. Your financial information stays completely private.

Technology

MoneyInFuture is built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no heavy frameworks, no npm bundles. This keeps the site fast, lightweight, and functional even on slow connections. The site is designed to work offline once loaded.

Calculator Accuracy

All financial formulas are based on standard Indian investment conventions (CAGR-equivalent monthly compounding, annuity-due SIP timing, standard EMI formula). Calculations include clear assumptions. Where approximations are used (e.g., yearly compounding of monthly SIPs), this is documented in the calculator's assumptions section.
