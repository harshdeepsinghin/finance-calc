Understanding Reducing Interest Loans

EMIs are computed using a reducing balance method where interest is computed monthly on the outstanding loan balance.

Formula & Mathematical Approach

Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) for reducing-balance loans. 

Monthly Rate (r) = Annual Rate / 12 / 100
EMI = [P * r * (1 + r) ^ Months] / [(1 + r) ^ Months - 1]

Key Assumptions

  • Interest rate remains fixed during the loan tenure.
  • Payments are made at the end of each month.
  • No prepayments or penalty fees are included.
