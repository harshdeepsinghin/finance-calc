The Financial Independence Timeline

Financial Independence is reached when your safe withdrawal rate (SWR) exceeds your annual living expenses adjusted for inflation.

Formula & Mathematical Approach

Timeline calculator showing years remaining to reach financial independence. 

Target Corpus = Annual Expenses * (100 / SWR)
Projects when net worth compounds to reach this target.

Key Assumptions

  • Annual savings and expenses increase with inflation.
  • Safe Withdrawal Rate (SWR) is constant.
