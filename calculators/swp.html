Retirement Planning with SWP

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) allows you to withdraw a fixed amount of money regularly from your mutual fund investments, while the remaining balance continues to compound.

Formula & Mathematical Approach

A Systematic Withdrawal Plan (SWP) allows you to withdraw a fixed amount regularly from an accumulated corpus. 

i = (1 + Return Rate / 100) ^ (1 / 12) - 1
Balance after withdrawal = (Current Balance - Monthly Withdrawal) * (1 + i)

Key Assumptions

  • Withdrawals are executed at the beginning of each month (annuity-due).
  • Interest is applied to the remaining balance after the monthly withdrawal.
  • The return rate is steady and withdrawals remain level.
