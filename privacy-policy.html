Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Overview

MoneyInFuture is built around a simple principle: your financial data is yours. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal or financial information that you enter into our calculators.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • Investment amounts, income, or expense figures you enter
  • Calculator inputs or outputs
  • Personal identification information
  • Device identifiers or fingerprints
  • Browsing history on this site

Cookies

MoneyInFuture does not use tracking cookies. We store your display preferences (dark mode, show/hide sliders, etc.) in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any third-party analytics service. There are no tracking scripts on this website.

Third-Party Libraries

Some pages may load MathJax (a mathematical formula renderer) from a CDN when TeX formulas are present. This is the only external network request made by the site. MathJax does not collect personal data. All other JavaScript (engine.js, shared.js, html2canvas, jsPDF) is served from the same domain.

Local Storage

We store the following data in your browser's localStorage, purely for improving your experience:

  • theme — your preferred light/dark mode
  • moneyinfuture_user_prefs — display preferences (sliders, words, color coding, etc.)
  • moneyinfuture_shared_prefs — last-used return rate, years, and inflation rate
  • sidebar-collapsed — sidebar layout preference

You can clear this data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for this domain.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the site evolves. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
